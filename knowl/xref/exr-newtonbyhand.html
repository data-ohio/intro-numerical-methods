Exercise 2.

For \(f(x) = x^{2}- 5\text{,}\) perform 3 iterations of Newton’s method with starting point \(x_{0}= 2\text{.}\) (By hand, but use a calculator.) Show all steps. Calculate the solution (\(x^{*}= \sqrt{5}\)) on a calculator and find the errors and percentage errors of \(x_{0}\text{,}\) \(x_{1}\text{,}\) \(x_{2}\) and \(x_{3}\text{.}\) Use enough digits so that you do not falsely conclude the error is zero.
